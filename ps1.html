Play Station 1

Do you remember this magnificent console?, for me it's something special, leading me to an eternal love with Sony's console brand and meeting most of my favorites franchises. Many games comes to my mind, but the most I remember is that initial sound when you turn on the console.

Startup sound

Don't worry, the attached sound is for a successful startup. We all know about running a game (sometimes a not so legal copy), and crossing fingers waiting for the PSX to read it and being able to play. For those nostalgics, we also have a video of how Sony Interactive Entertainment publicity wanted to gain new public from Nintendo and Sega, as the new contender entered (and won), into the fifth generation consoles war.

For our team, PSX brought the 3D games (and 2.5D, as well), to our life. Here is a list of the most popular games for our crew members.

  1. Final Fantasy VII (Square)
  2. Resident Evil 2 (Capcom)
  3. Metal Gear Solid (Konami)
  4. Castlevania: Symphony of the night (Konami)
  5. Crash Bandicoot: Warped (Naughty Dog)
